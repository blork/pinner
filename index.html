Pinner

A delightful app for your Pinboard bookmarks

Also on Android

Features

Share Extensions

For the first time, create a new bookmark in Pinner from anywhere you can share a URL or web page, using the new Share Extensions available in iOS 8.

Quickly save a webpage to Pinboard without ever leaving the current app. Like to automate your apps? Pinner also supports a URL scheme.

Continuity

Keep reading a bookmark when switching between devices using Handoff.

Transfer a bookmark directly between copies of the app running on iPhone & iPad, or send the page to Safari on devices where Pinner isn't installed - including Macs running OS X Yosemite.

Apple Watch

Pinner for Apple Watch brings bookmark discovery to your wrist. Quickly check out the popular and recent bookmarks from Pinboard.

Search for bookmark tags using your voice. And see bookmarks from your network - from other Pinboard users you follow. Check out the preview video to see it in action.

Social Bookmarking

Along with your bookmarks - synced and saved to your device - browse through categories of bookmarks from other users, including Popular, Recent, Wikipedia & Fandom.

Follow users on Pinboard.in, then view their latest bookmarks through the Network section.

Customisation

Create custom searches on both your bookmarks and community bookmarks.

Get fast access to your most important bookmarks & the newest ones from around the web. Filter by tag, title, author and URL.

Optimised

Optimised mode, powered by Readability, gives you just the article and cuts out the cruft. Now with improved support for Pinboard notes and animated GIFs.

Adjust the font, text size, margins, line spacing - create the perfect reading experience.

Theming

Triple-tap the top bar to switch to Dark Mode, designed to be perfect for night-time reading.

Choose between one of 3 app fonts - Lato, Avenir, and the classic Helvetica Neue. Pick one of 15 fonts for reading.

All Devices

Pinner is a universal app available on iPhone and iPad, optimised for Retina displays, 64-bit CPUs, and all sizes of device - including the new iPhone 6 & iPhone 6 Plus.

AutoLayout & Dynamic Type support means Pinner looks great on every size of device, with a landscape mode that takes advantage of the iPhone 6 Plus' extra screen space.
