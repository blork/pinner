Pinner

A delightful app for your Pinboard bookmarks.

Tumblr App Store Help URL Scheme

Features

Share Extensions

Create a new bookmark in Pinner from anywhere you can share a URL or web page, using the new Share Extension.

Continuity

Handoff bookmarks from iPhone to iPad to Mac with Continuity.

Customisation

Create custom searches to give you fast access to your most important bookmarks. Filter by tag, title and URL.

Social Bookmarking

Along with your bookmarks, synced and saved to your device, browse through categories of bookmarks from other users, including Popular, Recent, Wikipedia & Fandom.

Follow users on Pinboard.in, then view their latest bookmarks through the Network section.
